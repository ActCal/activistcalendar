    Welcome to Activist Calendar! We are a network of Greater-Boston organizers, artivists, hacktivists and engaged community members working for a more just world. We aim to strengthen our community with co-created tools and programming that support collaborations and resource-building.

We are active in posting on social media public Boston-area events that we find out about through our networks.

With gratitude - ActCal organizers.

    The mission of Activist Calendar is to strengthen networks of organizers, artivists, hacktivists and engaged community members with tools and programming that support collaborations and community resource-building.

We do this through our online calendar (this website), social media (Facebook and Twitter) and in-person events (in development).
